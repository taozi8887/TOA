## ✨ TOA v0.3.3 - Timing Feedback + Fairer Early Clicks

**Gameplay & Timing:**
• Box now **shakes exactly when the tile reaches it** (level-timing driven), even if you miss or don't click at all
• Added a brief **spawn lockout** so a note can’t be judged instantly the moment it appears on screen
• Slightly **stricter 300 window** (more precise “perfect” timing)

**Bug Fixes:**
• Resolved remaining **hitsound**, **hitcheck**, and **ghost-click** edge cases (more consistent timing + audio feedback)
• Fixed **autoplay** not playing the normal hitsound
